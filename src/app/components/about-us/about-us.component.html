Trust in Action: About SNMBCT

Shri Navi Mumbai Bhanushali Charitable Trust (SNMBCT) is a dedicated community-based organization formed to serve and uplift the Bhanushali community in Navi Mumbai. Established in [Year], the Trust was founded with a shared vision: to preserve our rich cultural heritage while empowering individuals and families through education, support, and unity.

We focus on initiatives that strengthen the social, educational, and economic foundations of the Bhanushali community. Our key programs include educational support for students, health awareness and check-up camps, and community events that promote cultural pride and togetherness.

The Trust is guided by a proactive Core Committee, which provides strategic direction and oversight. To serve different segments of the community effectively, SNMBCT operates through three dynamic teams, each dedicated to specific age groups and focus areas:

Asmita – Focused on the empowerment and well-being of women and senior citizens. This team organizes initiatives such as Women’s Day celebrations and Maytarno Medavo, a heartfelt program dedicated to honoring senior members of the community. Asmita continues to expand its efforts with new initiatives aimed at fostering inclusion, support, and personal growth for women and elders alike.

Ameya – A vibrant and energetic team dedicated to youth between the ages of 18 to 30. Ameya conducts various developmental, cultural, and community-driven activities designed to inspire leadership, self-growth, and social responsibility among young adults.

Sanskar Dham – Centered around children and adolescents, Sanskar Dham promotes cultural, moral, and religious values. Through engaging programs and interactive experiences, the team helps instill pride in heritage and encourages the next generation to stay rooted in tradition while growing with confidence.

A major highlight of 2025 was Sanskruti Utsav, a grand cultural program jointly organized by Asmita, Ameya, and Sanskar Dham. The event featured over 200 children performing on a large stage, presenting vibrant and meaningful acts based on religious themes. It was a proud moment for the community, showcasing unity, talent, and deep-rooted cultural values.

Rooted in the values of service, solidarity, and self-improvement, SNMBCT is committed to fostering a supportive environment where every member of the Bhanushali community can thrive.

With the collective efforts of the Core Committee, sub-teams, volunteers, donors, and community leaders, we strive to create meaningful and lasting change—preserving our identity while building a brighter, more connected future for generations to come.
